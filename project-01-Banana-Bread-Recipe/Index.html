Grandma's Banana Bread Recipe

Quick and easy recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 Bananas
  • 200grs of Butter (lightly Softened)
  • 1 cups of Sugar
  • 2 cups of Self-rising flour
  • 2 eggs
  • May need to add water/milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F or 180C, or a gas oven to about medium heat.
  2. Use a fork and mash the bananas.
  3. Add the lightly softened butter and mash it with together with the bananas.
  4. Add the cup of sugar and mix with the fork.
  5. Slowly add flour while mixing.
  6. Add 1 egg at a time.
  7. Observe the texture, it should be roughly smooth and thick maybe a little glumpy.
  8. Add water/milk if needed in little amounts.
  9. Add the mixture to the pan and place in preheated oven.
  10. Bake for 30 to 40 mins.
  11. Remove and let cool before removing from pan.
